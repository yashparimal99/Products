About Sending Money Abroad

Sending money abroad, also known as outward remittance, allows individuals to transfer funds from India to recipients in other countries for various purposes like family maintenance, education, or gifts. These transfers can be done through banks or specialized money transfer services, often with features like online tracking and guaranteed exchange rates. The process involves verifying details, transferring funds through banking networks, and currency conversion, ultimately delivering the funds to the recipient.
