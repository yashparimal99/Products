How Your Debit Card Works

Physical Card Usage
  1. Insert or swipe your card at merchant POS terminals
  2. Select 'Savings' or 'Current' account type when prompted
  3. Enter your 4-digit PIN to authorize the transaction
  4. Collect your receipt after successful payment
Online Transactions
  1. Select 'Debit Card' as payment method at checkout
  2. Enter your 16-digit card number, expiry date, and CVV
  3. Authenticate with the OTP sent to your registered mobile
  4. Receive confirmation of your successful transaction
