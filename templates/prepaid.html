How To Get & Use Your Prepaid Card

Getting Your Card
  1. Choose the prepaid card type that suits your needs
  2. Apply online or visit any Digi Bank branch
  3. Complete minimal KYC documentation
  4. Load funds onto your card (varies by card type)
  5. Receive your activated card within 3-5 business days
Using Your Card
  1. Check your available balance online or via SMS
  2. Swipe/insert at merchant locations (enter PIN when prompted)
  3. For online purchases, enter card details and complete transaction
  4. Reload your card as needed (where applicable)
  5. Track all transactions through your account statements

Frequently Asked Questions

  • Prepaid Cards: Loaded with funds in advance, no bank account needed, no credit facility
  • Debit Cards: Linked to your bank account, spends directly from your balance
  • Credit Cards: Borrow money up to a limit, repay later with possible interest

Reload options vary by card type:
  • Meal Cards: Can be reloaded by the employer/organization
  • Gift Cards: Typically non-reloadable (single-use)
  • Smart Pay Cards: Can be reloaded multiple times through net banking or bank branches

Digi Bank Prepaid Cards may have:
  • One-time issuance fees (varies by card type)
  • Reload fees for Smart Pay Cards
  • Inactivity fees after prolonged non-use
  • ATM withdrawal charges (if applicable)
  • No fees for regular POS transactions
Specific fee details are provided at the time of application.
