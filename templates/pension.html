Pension Disbursement Account

DiGi Bank Pension Disbursement Account

Manage your pension payments with ease and enjoy convenient banking services with DiGi Bank's Pension Disbursement Account. The Pension Disbursement Account simplifies banking for individuals earning pension. Its benefits include latest banking solutions at low costs. All you have to do is open the account using the required documents before getting your first pension and provide a Life Certificate issued by the authority of the organisation you had served earlier.

Pensioners can now download 'DIRGHAYU' Mobile App from Google Play Store (Android), Apple Store (iOS) or CPAO's website https://cpao.nic.in. This app provides details of last 24 pension disbursements as well as a facility for raising grievances (if any).
